The Best Movies According to Nayan

My top 3 movies of all time

"3 Idiots"

"3 Idiots" is a 2009 Indian Hindi-language coming-of-age comedy-drama film directed by Rajkumar Hirani. The story follows three engineering students—Rancho, Farhan, and Raju—at the prestigious Imperial College of Engineering. Through their friendship, the film explores themes of the pressures of the educational system, the value of following one's passion, and the meaning of success. The film is both humorous and poignant, offering a critique of rote learning and the relentless pursuit of grades, while celebrating creativity and originality. It stars Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, Sharman Joshi, Kareena Kapoor, and Boman Irani.

"Charlie"

"Charlie" is a 2015 Indian Malayalam-language romantic drama film directed by Martin Prakkat and starring Dulquer Salmaan (DQ) and Parvathy Thiruvothu. The story revolves around Tessa, a graphic artist who moves into a rented house and discovers the intriguing and whimsical life of its previous tenant, Charlie, through his left-behind belongings. Fascinated by his adventurous and free-spirited lifestyle, Tessa embarks on a journey to find Charlie, leading to a series of enchanting encounters and a deeper understanding of life and love. The film is known for its vibrant visuals, captivating music, and the chemistry between the lead actors.

"KGF chapter 1"

"KGF: Chapter 1" is a 2018 Indian Kannada-language period action film directed by Prashanth Neel. The movie stars Yash in the lead role as Raja Krishnappa Bairya "Rocky" and features Srinidhi Shetty, Ramachandra Raju, and Anant Nag in key roles. Set in the 1970s and early 1980s, the film follows Rocky's rise from poverty to power in the Kolar Gold Fields (KGF), a region notorious for its vast reserves of gold. The narrative interweaves Rocky's quest for power with the oppressive conditions of the gold mines, highlighting his determination to become the most powerful and influential figure in the region. The film is notable for its high-octane action sequences, engaging storyline, and compelling performances. "KGF: Chapter 1" received widespread acclaim and became a massive box office success, setting the stage for its sequel, "KGF: Chapter 2."
